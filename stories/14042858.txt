MailShell is a daemon that allows you to execute shell commands over your email. You add your credentials to the and fields at the top of mailshell.rb file and execute the daemon (It would be a good idea generate a application specific password to use with MailShell). You might need to run .

MailShell currently only works with a Gmail account.

MailShell will then parse the command, execute it, and then email you the output of the command. It does not matter what the body of the email contains.

MailShell looks for command emails in your email account every two minutes.

MailShell will delete the command email after sending you the output.
